Department of mathematics came into subsistence in 2010 as a separate module and flourished as one of the most prominent branch of the institute comprising of two well learned and research oriented faculty members (PhD from premier institutes). The department has evolved steadily during the past few years and it offers core courses to undergraduate and postgraduate students from different engineering branches. Recently in 2014 the department also introduces doctoral programme which elicits student for performing research in the areas of both pure and applied mathematics.

The department has pioneered in organizing workshops and seminars intended to improve the potential and quality of teaching and research for students and faculty members..
