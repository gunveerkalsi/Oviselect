Facilities

The placement office is well equipped and is designed to smoothly handle and support the placement procedure at every stage. We are equipped with the following facilities to aid the process:
  • State of the art guest house with A/C rooms.
  • Auditorium for pre-placement talks and seminars.
  • Fully furnished group discussion and interview rooms.
  • Fully computerized office.
  • Internet connected computer lab devoted to training and placement.
  • Student coordinators for any kind of assistance during visit.
  • Computer lab with 200+ systems interconnected by LAN and high speed internet for online tests.
