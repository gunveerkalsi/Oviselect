Office of Academics Affairs

Welcome to The Office of Academics

Academics Facilitates:

(a) Admissions

  1. Prospectus: preparation & printing;
  2. Sale of Application Forms;
  3. Issue of Admit Cards for Admission Test;
  4. Setting of Test Papers; Conduct of Admission Test; Evaluation of Answer Scripts;
  5. Announcement of Results;
  6. Admission Interviews / Counseling; and
  7. Issue of Admission Letters.

(b) Programs

  1. Course Registration; Semester Schedule;
  2. Scheduling of Mid- & End-semester examinations; Evaluation and Re-evaluation of exams;
  3. Grade records, Grade Cards, & Transcripts;
  4. Academic discipline & academic integrity;
  5. Campus Mentor Program (S-SM-FM interaction);
  6. Monitoring teaching and Faculty Member workload; and
  7. Student exchange; inter-areas of study transfer of students; Convocation; New Programs.
