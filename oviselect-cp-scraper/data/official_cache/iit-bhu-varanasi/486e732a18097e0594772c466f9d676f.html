    Director's Message

The Institute is committed to achieve excellence in research and innovation, with a focus towards the well-being of the society and solution to global issues. Faculty members, students and startup companies at IIT(BHU) Varanasi are engaged in cutting-edge research under various schemes. Specialized research facilities, such as Precision Engineering Hub, Central Instrument facility, and Supercomputing center, are equipped with the state of the art equipments to provide a creative and conducive research environment for the institute faculty and students, and other institutions and industries, in and around IIT (BHU). Furthermore, several innovation and research centers are built to tackle technological challenges that can be addressed by multi-disciplinary researchers. The institute has also set up Tinkering labs in various departments to nurture young minds in research projects. I hope the R&D activities at the Institute will help in the growth of the country by contributing towards the Make in India initiative of the Government of India.

     Dean's Message

IIT (BHU) Varanasi has developed a research and innovation friendly environment supported through state of the art infrastructure at Department/School level, as well as advanced Central Instrumentation Facility and Precision Engineering Hub. The Institute strongly encourages industry-academia partnership and international collaboration of mutual interests. Research activities at IIT (BHU) Varanasi are aligned with the national mission of Swachh Bharat, Ayushman Bharat, Make in India, Digital India, Atal Innovation Mission etc. These challenges are best served by multi-disciplinary research using concepts of Science, Engineering and Technology.

 

The Institute has proven expertise in the areas of steel, advanced materials, microwave technology, electrical and electronic devices, artificial intelligence, composite materials, novel reactor design, new drugs, and sensors/biosensors. The Institute has identified thrust areas of Research in Green and Clean Energy, Hydrogen Energy, Environment and Water, Healthcare, Bioengineering, Biomedical Devices, Artificial Intelligence, Precision Engineering, Material Science and Defense. The Research and Development outcome of the Institute has growing impact in recent years under the visionary leadership of the Institute Director. We thank government agencies and industry partners for relying on our research competence.

The Research and Development (R&D) office plays an important role in the Institute's reputation, ranking and forming relationships with government and private funding agencies. The office also facilitates international collaborations with educational Institutions and Industries. As Dean (R&D) of the Institute, I am sensitive to my role and responsibilities. I give my best effort for continuous improvement, through participation of all faculty members and Heads/Coordinators of Departments/Schools, to foster the research attitude and innovation process. The office of Dean (R&D) will not leave any stone unturned to meet expectations of the Institute and all stakeholders. We, as team IIT (BHU) Varanasi, are committed to contribute towards shaping India as a knowledge and innovation based economy.
