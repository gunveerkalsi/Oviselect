Mission

To become a center of excellence pioneering in education, research & development, and leaders in design & manufacturing.

Vision

To become a leading institute of higher learning in Information Technology enabled design & manufacturing to create technologies and technologists befitting the industries globally.

Charter

To carry out advanced research and development activities in design and manufacturing technologies, both on its own and on sponsorship basis for the industry.
