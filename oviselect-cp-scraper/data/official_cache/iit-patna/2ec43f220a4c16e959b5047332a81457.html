CAMPUS LIFE of IIT PATNA

Since 2008... Always ready, always alert, never retreating.

IIT PATNAWELCOMES YOU

We are excited to have you as part of our community. Here, you’ll find a place to grow, explore, and reach new height. Embrace the opportunities ahead, and let’s create an environment of learning, collaboration, and success together.

Live Events

Stay connected with the heartbeat of our campus! Don't miss out on the thrill of our live events — from vibrant cultural festivals and student celebrations to engaging workshops, competitions, and groundbreaking showcases. There's always something happening, and we're bringing it to you in real time!

Campus Eateries & Dining

Fuel your studies and connect with friends at our diverse range of campus eateries. From hearty mess meals and late-night canteen snacks to bustling cafeterias, we offer a variety of options to suit every taste and schedule.

Sports & Recreational Facilities

Stay active and competitive with our extensive sports facilities. Whether you prefer team sports, individual training, or recreational activities, our campus provides the grounds, courts, and equipment to pursue your passion.

Healthcare & Wellness

Your health and well-being are our priority. Our campus is equipped with comprehensive healthcare services also a larger hospital adjacent to the Institute, including a dedicated health center, emergency support, and counseling services to ensure you stay healthy and supported.

Key Campus Amenities

Our campus provides essential physical amenities designed for a conducive learning and living experience, including comfortable accommodation, modern academic spaces, and areas for student activities.
