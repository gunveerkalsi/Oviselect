Vision:

Innovation and Sustainability: Focus on continuous innovation in technology, with an emphasis on sustainability and environmentally friendly solutions.

Global Connectivity and Automation: Creating a connected world through advanced telecommunications and automation technologies to improve efficiency and quality of life.

Quality of Life and Global Challenges: Enhancing the quality of life through accessible, inclusive technology and addressing global challenges like climate change and energy crises.

Mission:

Innovation: Drive technological advancements and create new systems to enhance communication, energy efficiency, and overall quality of life.

Sustainable Solutions: Develop eco-friendly technologies and renewable energy sources to reduce environmental impact and combat climate change.

Safety and Security: Design systems to ensure the safety and security of critical infrastructure and electronic products.

Research and Development: Continuously advance technology through research, education, and international collaboration.

Adaptation and Resilience: Address emerging challenges and disruptions by designing resilient electrical and electronic systems that can withstand extreme conditions.

Programmes:

The success of our department is to produce the following results:

  • Teaching students the fundamentals of electrical engineering so that they can go on to become successful professionals in the field.

  • Attempting to push the boundaries of knowledge by conducting innovative studies in several subfields of electrical engineering.

  • Students are equipped for professions in power systems, power electronics, control system and other fields after graduation.

  • Getting students ready to take the necessary exams to earn their professional licenses and certificates.

  • Aiding in the Progress of Societal Needs in the Fields of Sustainable Energy, Better Communication Systems, and More.

  • Fostering an environment that fosters creativity and initiative in both students and teachers.

  • Working together with other academic institutions and businesses to advance electrical engineering research and development is our seventh priority.

  • Providing Post-Graduate and Working Engineers with Opportunities for Lifelong Learning and Professional Development.

  • Community Engagement Working with locals and businesses to solve problems in the real world.

  • Leadership development that produces influential figures in the electrical engineering sector and adjacent businesses is priority.

Message from the Director

Welcome to the Department of Electrical Engineering, IIIT Bhubaneswar.

Our faculty is especially proud of the pioneering electrical engineering students and researchers it has trained in the past and will train in the future. Students in Electrical Engineering and related cutting-edge research fields have been given a solid technical grounding thanks to the institution's well-established teaching and research environment.

The B.Tech students in this area have access to a wide variety of electives, including both professional and general options. The primary goal is to give students a solid foundation in fundamental electrical and electronics concepts, on top of which they can expand by studying more narrowly focused topics with greater intensity. Academic research, independent/group projects, and laboratory courses help students acquire the analytical, design, and experimental skills necessary for success in a wide range of disciplines. The program allows for practical experience through project work and facilitates employment with reputable businesses.

Research innovation is heavily encouraged at the doctoral level, and students are given the tools they need to pursue careers in academia, industry, and government.

We invite you to be part of our journey as we strive to propel our department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering to  ever-greater heights.

Sincerely, and with best wishes,
