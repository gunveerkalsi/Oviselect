About Us

Nestled on the banks of the sacred Ganges, Varanasi stands as one of the world’s oldest living cities — a timeless confluence of knowledge, culture, and spirituality. For millennia, it has drawn seekers, scholars, and travelers in pursuit of wisdom and enlightenment. The ghats, temples, and narrow lanes of the city narrate tales of resilience, continuity, and harmony, where ancient traditions coexist effortlessly with the modern spirit. The city’s rhythm intertwines the contemplative calm of its heritage with the vibrancy of contemporary life, making it not merely a destination, but an enduring experience — one that invites reflection, learning, and transformation. At the spiritual heart of this historic city stands the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, it is a beacon of engineering and technological excellence. IIT (BHU) Varanasi’s heritage dates back to 1919 with the establishment of the Banaras Engineering College (BENCO) by the visionary Mahamana Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya. After evolving through the years, the institution achieved the full status of an Indian Institute of Technology in 2012, cementing its place among the top IITs in India. Set within the magnificent campus of the historic Banaras Hindu University, the institute offers world-class Bachelors, Masters, and Ph.D. programmes across diverse engineering, science, and humanities departments. The institute is committed to foster a culture of innovation, research, and entrepreneurship, thereby preparing students to tackle a wide range of challenges. The institute also has a strong alumni and global network that pioneers legacy. Driven by a vision of global engagement and inclusivity, the Office of International Relations aims to position IIT (BHU) as a center for international collaboration and innovation. Through its initiatives, the office aspires to cultivate a diverse, connected academic community where ideas transcend borders, research addresses global challenges, and partnerships create meaningful impact. In doing so, it upholds the institute’s commitment to excellence — empowering IIT (BHU) and the city of Varanasi to shine together on the world stage as beacons of knowledge, culture, and progress

Prof. Amit Patra

Director's Message

The Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, has a glorious tradition of over a hundred years. This institute stands at the crossroads where tradition meets technology and transformation. It has continuously redefined the frontiers of education and research through innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and global engagement. International collaboration in higher education plays an increasingly important role in today's interconnected world. Our focus is to bridge the gap between the global academic community and our institute by encouraging student and faculty exchanges, joint research programs, and international internships that enhance both academic and cultural learning through a well-developed network with universities, research institutes, and industries across continents. At IIT(BHU), internationalization is a philosophy embedded in our pursuit of excellence. We aim to create a welcoming and vibrant international ecosystem on campus. We take pride in hosting students and scholars from around the world and enabling our own community to gain enriching experiences abroad. We warmly welcome our global partners to collaborate with us in driving innovation, advancing knowledge, and shaping a sustainable future for humanity.

Prof. Santosh K Singh

Professor In-charge’s Message
It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the International Relation’s portal of IIT (BHU), Varanasi. The institute has always envisioned a globally connected academic environment that fosters collaboration, research, and cultural exchange. IIT (BHU) reaches beyond borders through international relationships in pursuit of strong ties with top-rated universities and research organizations around the world. These collaborations facilitate the exchange of students and faculties, joint research works, and global learning opportunities that enrich the academic and research ecosystem. The student team of IR also plays an important connecting link between the international collaborators and the institute community, aids in outreach programs, and helps in bringing awareness about global academic opportunities to the students. We invite you to explore this platform to learn more about our international engagements and opportunities.
