Vision:

The vision of the department is to produce professionals in Electronics and Telecom Engineering through education and research and collaborate with the Industry by learning from and contributing to the existing engineering technology and practices.

Mission:

The Mission of the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is:

  • To educate students in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
  • To develop analysis and synthesis skills among the students.
  • To develop passion for excellence and a questioning mindset among students.
  • To develop culture of self-learning to be life-long learners.
  • To be in touch with Industrial practices and contribute to the same through research and development.
  • To address problems faced by industry and government through consultancy.
Programmes:

After completion of the course, B. Tech Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering graduates will have:

  1. Ability to apply knowledge of mathematics, computer science, and related areas of science and engineering to the fields of Electronics hardware and software.
  2. Ability to design and conduct experiments, as well as to organize, analyze and interpret data.
  3. Ability to design hardware and software systems, components, or processes to meet identified needs within realistic constraints such as economic, environmental, social, ethical, health and safety, manufacturability, and sustainability.
  4. Ability to work in a team with self responsibility, mutual cooperation and respect.
  5. Ability to identify, formulate and solve hardware and software problems using engineering principles and practices.
  6. To understand professional, legal, and ethical responsibility in engineering practice.
  7. Ability to communicate effectively by oral, written, and graphical means.
  8. Ability to engage in lifelong learning, and to independently acquire required information to solve problem.
  9. Ability to work on complex research problems using engineering fundamentals.
  10. Ability to use the techniques, skills, and computing tools necessary for electronics engineering practice.

Additionally, graduates of the department will also:

  1. Develop a spirit of enquiry
  2.  Develop ability to work in inter-disciplinary and multi-cultural environment
