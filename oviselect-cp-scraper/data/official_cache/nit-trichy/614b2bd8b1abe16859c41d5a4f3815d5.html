    Architecture

     

     

     

    VISION

    To create a world class learning environment to meet the challenges of built environment

    MISSION

    • Create professionally competent and socially responsible architects with human values
    • Enhance knowledge and skill through academic, research and application
    • Serve the society through meaningful interactions

     

    MILESTONES OF THE DEPARTMENT

    1980- Department was started with an intake of 15 students for the B.Arch Programme, affiliation to Madras University (accommodated in the then Physics block)

    1982- Affiliation to Bharathidasan University

    1983- Architecture department moved to a separate building – present Telecom block

    1986- Autonomous status granted by Bharathidasan University, Intake increased to 30

    1989- Moved to Silver Jubilee building

    2003- REC became NIT with Deemed University status, PhD programme started in Architecture dept.

    2004 – Established exclusive  Computer lab for the department

    2010- Moved to current building, designed by Prof. Joseph Newman Fernando

    2014 – Refurbished Computer lab

    2015- Intake increased to 60

    2016- M.Arch Programme in Energy Efficient & Sustainable Architecture started with an intake of 20 students

    2018- Annexe block  & 2nd floor were added (Architects Dr. G. Subbaiyan & Dr.P. Gopalakrishnan)
