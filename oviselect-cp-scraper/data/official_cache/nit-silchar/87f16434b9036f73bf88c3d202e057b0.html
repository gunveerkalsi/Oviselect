    About the Department

    Established in 2012, the Department of Management Studies (DoMS) at NIT Silchar has been delivering transformative management education for 14 years. Located on a 700-acre campus with dedicated facilities, it offers modern infrastructure, including digital classrooms, analytics and research labs, and a departmental library. The MBA program provides specialisations in Marketing, Finance, and Human Resources, combining theory with practical exposure through case studies, industrial visits, and a mandatory 45-day internship. Emphasising holistic development, the department fosters professional skills via academic and extracurricular activities. Guided by experienced faculty, DoMS has produced successful alumni who excel as business leaders and entrepreneurs worldwide.

    Our Vision

    The vision of NIT-DoMS is to be a centre of excellence where the synergies of technology and management will be blended to serve the global challenges of business and industry in the 21st century's dynamic business environment.

    To impart quality knowledge and modern skills through innovation and continuous development of the competencies and transforming the budding youngsters into leaders of the corporate world and creating knowledge pool through frontier research.
