Department of Chemistry

The Department of Chemistry was established in 1960 and Prof. R. P. Jeerath was the founder Head of the Department. After Prof. Jeerath, in 1975, The Department was guided by Prof. Ram Murthi, followed by Prof. Shakti Rais Ahmad, Prof. A. K. Choudhry, Prof. A. R. Shah, Prof A. S. Bali, Prof. Siraj Ahmad, Prof. S. A. Shah, Prof. Tabassum Ara, Prof. J. A. Banday, Prof. Kowsar Kowsar Majid and Prof. Hamida-Tun-Nisa Chisti. Presently, it is being run under the headship of Prof. Kowsar Majid. More than 40 Ph.Ds and seven M.Phils have been produced by the Department so far. The faculty members of the Department have published many scientific papers and reviews in National and International journals of repute. Research, in the areas of Physical, Organic, Inorganic, Environmental chemistry, Synthetic Organic Chemistry, Nanomaterials, Biofuels and Electrochemistry are being carried out in the Department.
