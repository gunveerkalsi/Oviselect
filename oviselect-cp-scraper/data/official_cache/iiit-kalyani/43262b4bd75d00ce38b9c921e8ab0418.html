About IIIT Kalyani

IIIT Kalyani is committed to becoming a Center of Excellence in IT education and research, preparing students to meet the industry’s evolving demands with innovation and expertise. The institute actively fosters an entrepreneurial ecosystem, driving new technological solutions and intellectual property development within the State.

Established through a Not-for-Profit Public-Private Partnership (N-PPP), IIIT Kalyani is funded by the Government of India (Ministry of Education), the Government of West Bengal, and industry partners in a 50:35:15 ratio. The West Bengal government has also contributed 50 acres of land for its permanent campus. Current industry partners include Coal India Ltd. and the Rolta Foundation.
