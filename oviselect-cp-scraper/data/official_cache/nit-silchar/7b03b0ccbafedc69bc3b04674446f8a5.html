    About Us

    The Department of Mathematics was established along with the inception of the Institute and has since been committed to providing high-quality technical education while offering strong foundational support to all engineering disciplines. Over the years, the department has consistently fostered analytical thinking, academic excellence, and a spirit of professionalism among its students.

    With a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculty members, the department strives to maintain high standards in teaching and research. It is also supported by well-equipped laboratories and a conducive academic environment that promotes innovation, collaboration, and the effective learning of mathematical concepts essential for modern science and engineering.

    Our Vision

    The vision of the Department of Mathematics, National Institute of Technology Silchar is as follows:.

    1. Our Department is to be recognized nationally and internationally for its excellence in teaching and research.
    2. Our Department should be remembered in providing excellent Mathematical skills to its UG, PG and Ph. D. students.
    3. Department is to create a platform for encouraging outcome based research environment.

    The mission of the Department of Mathematics, National Institute of Technology Silchar is as follows:

    1. To excel in providing high quality Mathematical skills to undergraduate, post graduate and Ph. D. students.
    2. To provide in significant research.
    3. To attend the mathematical needs of the institute and the community.
    4. To provide students (UG/PG) with a wide spectrum of valuable courses with rigorous training/tutorials that enables them to pursue their future.
    5. To make Engineers to develop the mathematical thoughts and applying it to solve complex engineering problems, designing mathematical models to meet the requirements.
    6. To encourage both research scholars and faculty members of the Department to be the exposures of different research environments in India and abroad.

    Academics & Program

    POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMME (M. Sc.)

    The M.Sc. program in Mathematics provides advanced knowledge in various areas of Mathematics. The program is designed such that a student can compete it based on advanced coursework alone. However, the students are given the option to concentrate on a problem by substituting some of the courses with equivalent project work.

    The M. Sc. is a two-year program, where all four semesters are spent doing theory, computation, and project & dissertation work. A project & dissertation work commences in the last semester of the program. The project typically has a strong research component.

    DOCTOR OF PHILOSOPHY (Ph. D.)

    The Ph.D. program is a post-graduate program focused on original research, that can lead to publications in premier conferences or journals. It is the highest level of degree that a student can achieve. Students are required to do some courses in the first few semesters (course work required can vary based on student background), but soon move on completely to research. All Ph.D. Scholars learn teaching skills during tutorial classes with faculties in B.Tech. courses. After completing the Ph.D. program the scholar is capable of understanding significant particulars of Mathematics.
