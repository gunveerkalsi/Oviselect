About NIT Sikkim

NIT Sikkim is a prestigious institution providing quality education and a dynamic academic curriculum in Engineering, Science, and Humanities, offering 17 courses across nine departments, including B.Tech., M.Tech., M.Sc., and PhD programs. The institute focuses on holistic student development and instils responsible citizenship by promoting inclusivity, encouraging exploration, and supporting extracurricular activities to foster students' passions and interests.

Placement Statistics

The Training and Placement Cell of NIT Sikkim is breaking its placement record for the last three years and setting a new milestone. In batch 2019-2023, the placement record stood at the highest, achieving 83.70% from a commendable statistic of 80.84% in the 2017-2021 batch. NIT Sikkim has achieved a remarkable milestone with its current placement statistics, soaring to 75.37%. These outstanding achievements reflects the institute's commitment to fostering successful career opportunities for its students.
