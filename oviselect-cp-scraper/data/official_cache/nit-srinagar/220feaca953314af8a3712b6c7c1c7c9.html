Welcome to Central Research Facility Centre

Central Research Facility Center (CRFC) of National Institute of Technology Srinagar was established in 2012 to cater to researchers' needs within and outside the Institute; users from academic institutes and industries enjoy the benefits of the sophisticated research and testing facilities. The state-of-the-art research facilities are regularly upgraded and maintained by suitably trained and skilled technical staff. CRFC is one of the best centres with high-end multiscale and multi-dimensional research and testing facilities in Jammu and Kashmir. We at CRFC are determined to provide consultancy and testing services of global standard to Industries, Institutes and other departments. CRFC is continuously evolving and extending the research and testing infrastructure to meet the demands of researchers and Industries.

For any feedback and suggestions, write to us at crfc@nitsri.ac.in.
