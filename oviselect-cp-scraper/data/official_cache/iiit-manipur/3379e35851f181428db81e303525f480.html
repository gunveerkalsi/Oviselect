International Relations Division

The primary role of the International Relations Division are as follows.
  • To foster international collaboration between foreign institutions and IIIT Senapati, Manipur, and to help define scope of such relationships through appropriate Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs).
  • Creating and maintaining connections between researchers and experts in the thrust areas of IIIT Senapati, Manipur
  • To provide an interface to facilitate joint exchange activities such as semester abroad and internship for students
  • To provide supports to the International students to attend programmes of IIIT Senapati, Manipur
  • To provide opportunities to the members of International organization (students/ teacher/ staff) to visit IIIT Senapati, Manipur in a variety of exchange programmes, as well as for members of IIIT Senapati, Manipur to travel to the partner institutes to carry out academic and research activities of their interest (based on the scope of the MoUs).
  • To provide exposure and the opportunity to the members of IIIT Senapati, Manipur to organize joint seminars and conduct workshops.
  • To serve as a point of contact on all matters pertaining to internationalization efforts of IIIT Senapati, Manipur, including admission of Non-Resident Indians (NRI)/ Foreign nationals/ People of Indian origin (PIO) with overseas institute/ organizations and Ministries of various Government and Non-government agencies of India and the Government of Manipur.
