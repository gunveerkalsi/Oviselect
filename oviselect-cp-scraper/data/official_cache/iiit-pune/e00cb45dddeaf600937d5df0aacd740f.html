Welcome to IIIT Pune

To address the challenges faced by the Indian IT industry and growth of the domestic IT market, the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India intends to establish twenty Indian Institutes of Information Technology(IIIT), on a Not-for-profit Public Private Partnership (N-PPP) basis. The partners in setting up the IllTs would be the Ministry of Education (MoE), Governments of the respective States where each lllT will be established, and the industry.
