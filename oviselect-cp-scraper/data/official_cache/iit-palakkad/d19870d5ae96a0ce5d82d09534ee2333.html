Research Programs

IIT Palakkad offers research programs leading to M.S and Ph.D in various disciplines. The institute also offers post doctoral fellowships for highly motivated researchers in several areas.

Master of Science (M.S.) by Research

  • Civil Engineering (from August 2017)
  • Computer Science & Engineering (from August 2017)
  • Data Science (from January 2022)
  • Electrical Engineering (from August 2017)
  • Mechanical Engineering (from August 2017)
  • Environmental Sciences and Sustainable Engineering Centre (from July 2022)
  • Biological Sciences & Engineering ( from January 2025 )
