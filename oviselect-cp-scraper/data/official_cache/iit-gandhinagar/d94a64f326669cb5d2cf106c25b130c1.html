IITGN launches AI Resilience & Command (ARC) Centre

As Indian cities grapple with increasingly frequent floods, climate extremes, and mounting infrastructure stress, the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IITGN) has launched its AI Resilience and Command (ARC) Centre on February 05, 2026. Located at the IITGN Research Park, the Centre promotes data-driven climate-risk management, combining flood forecasting, mobility analysis and AI-enabled decision support to help cities anticipate and respond to extreme events.

Homecoming 2025

Every December, IIT Gandhinagar alumni from around the world come together to celebrate their shared journey — to relive old memories, create new ones, and rediscover the Institute that shaped who they are today. Homecoming is a celebration of this collective experience. It is the laughter echoing through hostel corridors, the late-night brainstorming sessions that turned into life lessons, and the friendships that time could never fade.
