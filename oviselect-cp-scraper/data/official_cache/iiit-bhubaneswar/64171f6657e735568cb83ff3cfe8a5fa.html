Directors Message

I am deeply honored to serve as the Director of the International Institute of Information Technology Bhubaneswar. Since its establishment in 2006, the institute has consistently strived for excellence in the fields of education & research in Information Technology. Over the past fifteen years, IIIT Bhubaneswar has emerged as a prominent institution in the domain of Information Technology. We have been guided by a vision to offer high-quality technical education and to serve as a platform for cutting-edge scientific research. Our mission has always been to unlock the full potential of students and faculty members. In pursuit of this vision, IIIT Bhubaneswar has maintained a stellar record of academic contributions, excelling in teaching, research, and governance. Today, our institute stands poised to make substantial contributions to technology solutions and sustainability research, while paving the way for the sustainable growth of technological resources. We are nurturing the next generation of leaders who will continue on this path in the years to come. Our students come from diverse educational and cultural backgrounds, and our programs are meticulously designed to instill qualities such as innovation, leadership, teamwork, and a global perspective. IIIT Bhubaneswar has achieved an impressive track record in student recruitment. The educational experience we provide here not only focuses on technological excellence but also imparts a comprehensive understanding of the world. Our academic programs are under constant review and enhancement, incorporating the latest technological theories and practices. We employ state-of-the-art technologies for effective course delivery, with a strong emphasis on practical applications. Our aspiration is to position ourselves on the global stage alongside the world’s leading technical institutions. Our institute is dedicated to creating an environment that fosters innovation, creativity, and academic excellence among our students. We provide a supportive and challenging atmosphere that enables our students to grow and thrive. At IIIT Bhubaneswar, we strongly endorse interdisciplinary research and development, aiming to benefit both industry and society. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded leadership skills. We are confident that our collective efforts will yield remarkable achievements in the broader academic community. We anticipate substantial contributions in various aspects of information technology, including academic innovation, high-impact journal publications, citations, and technology development & transfer. I extend a warm welcome to all the stakeholders to engage on this platform and mutually benefit each other. I invite you to visit our lush green campus and discover the potential of our talented students and faculty members who aspire for nothing less than excellence and are unafraid of real-world challenges.
