News

  • 10 Jan 2026

    IIIT Vadodara, in collaboration with IIT Gandhinagar, successfully organized the One-Day Workshop under Phase II of the Rashtriya Karmayogi Large-Scale Jan Seva Program. The workshop trained 36 participants, focusing on capacity building, effective public service delivery, and strengthening citizen-centric governance.

  • 20 - 22 Nov 2025

    Presenting a part of their work as posters at Adani Global Indology Conclave 20-22 November 2025 , Varad Vyavhare (student of 4th Year B.Tech. in CSE program) received first prize in thematic area (Edutainment Sciences) and Ravi Prakash Singh (student of 1st Year M.Tech. in CSE program) received second prize in thematic area (Agricultural Science, Veterinary and animal husbandry).

  • * May 2025

    Our first-year student, Mr. Satyam Bharatbhai Aniyaliya, secured 3rd place under the ‘Reduce E-Waste’ category in the Ideas4Life initiative by the Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change. His idea (ID 1364) was recognized under Mission LiFE.
