Prof. Mishra's teaching and research interests include

  • RF & Microwave Circuits and System Design: Filters, Resonators, RADAR and Satellite Systems and Subsystems; IoT and Wearable Electronics
  • Antenna Design: Micro-strip Antennas, UWB Antennas, Array Antennas, High gain multi-layer antennas, MIMO, Beam-forming Antennas, DRAs. Beamforming Antenna, Active Antenna, RF energy harvesting 
  • Microwave Remote Sensing and Sensors: Radiometry, Characterization of electrical parameters of target using microwave techniques, Back-scattering coefficient, Emissivity & Brightness Temperature 
  • RF/Microwave Measurements: Antenna Characterization, Chip Testing, Dielectric Measurements.
