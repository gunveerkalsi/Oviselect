Message from the Director

NIT Mizoram began its journey in 2010 to achieve global renown in research and education. Since 2010, the Institute has progressed rapidly. Presently, there are about 850 students studying in various disciplines of Engineering, Sciences, Humanities and Social Sciences. The Institute is working untiringly to impart quality education that keeps pace with the latest advances in research and development activities.
