Dr. Girijesh Kumar Patel
Designation: Assistant Professor
Qualification: M.Sc., Ph.D. (IIT Roorkee), and Postdoctoral appointments: OSU, OR; USA-MCI, AL and TTUHSC, TX, USA.
Area of Interest:

Dr. Patel's lab is engaged in the uncovering of novel targets and the development of biomarker(s) for the early detection of cancer using different “omics” platforms. The core of their research delves into unraveling the intricate molecular mechanisms that drive cancer progression, lineage plasticity, cancer cell stemness, metastasis, and therapy resistance. Furthermore, Dr. Patel's team is delving into the role of extracellular vesicles, particularly exosomes, in facilitating intercellular communication within the intricate landscape of the tumor microenvironment (TME) and harnessing their potential in cancer diagnostic, prognostic, and therapeutic interventions.
